In Praise of Backyard Cowboy Camping - 08/01/2025

I crave being outdoors and on the trail, but sometimes it really isn't convenient to go on a quick overnighter in Harriman. Maybe it's a weekday night or you have plans the next day. But this is when backyard cowboy camping rocks. Going to bed with the crickets chirping and waking up with the sun and the sound of the birds is the perfect reset that my brain needs. Thus, tonight I'm about to backyard cowboy camp. I can't wait. You can't call it lame until you've tried it...
